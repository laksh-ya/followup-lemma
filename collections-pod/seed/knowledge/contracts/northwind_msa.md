# Master Services Agreement — Acme Financial Services & Northwind Tech Pvt. Ltd.

Effective date: 2025-01-01 · Account tier: Enterprise (VIP)

## Clause 4 — Fees and payment

4.1 Acme shall invoice Northwind monthly in arrears for services rendered.

4.2 **Payment terms are Net-45**: Northwind shall pay each undisputed invoice within
forty-five (45) calendar days of the invoice date.

4.3 Amounts not paid within the Net-45 window are overdue. A late-payment charge of 1.5%
per month may apply to undisputed overdue balances after a 15-day grace period.

4.4 Northwind operates on approved credit terms. Persistent non-payment (balances
overdue beyond 21 days) entitles Acme to place the account on a credit hold, requiring
advance payment for further work.

## Clause 5 — Disputes

5.1 Northwind shall raise any invoice dispute in writing within ten (10) days of the
invoice date, specifying the disputed line items.

5.2 Late-payment charges are suspended on the disputed portion until the dispute is
resolved in good faith.

## Clause 9 — Notices and escalation

9.1 Routine billing correspondence is sent to accounts@northwindtech.com.

9.2 Formal escalation, including any pre-legal notice, must additionally be addressed to
the Northwind finance controller and allow a minimum 24-hour response window before
further action.

## Clause 12 — Relationship

12.1 As an Enterprise (VIP) account, Northwind is entitled to a named finance contact at
Acme and a softened collections cadence consistent with the parties' ongoing
relationship.
